What is Liquid Nitrogen?

Liquid nitrogen is nitrogen in a liquid state at an extremely low temperature (-320°). Nitrogen actually makes up approximately 78% of the air you breathe. So dont worry, its completely safe! And according to food science experts, rapid freezing preserves the nutrients in food.
